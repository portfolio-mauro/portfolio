In a rare alliance between free time and academic deadlines, I took on a passion project for my Master’s in User Experience & Service Design. The mission? To reimagine the website of the digital marketing agency I currently call my workplace. With whispers of a potential redesign floating around the office, I thought, why not give them something worth gossiping about? Who knows—this concept might just turn those rumors into reality!
